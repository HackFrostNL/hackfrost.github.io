Welcome to Hack Frost!

Hack Frost NL is a tech hackathon for students organized by students from Memorial University of Newfoundland. We invite students to participate in an exciting weekend to learn new skills and connect with a community of tech enthusiasts! No matter your experience level, this hackathon is for YOU! Come chill with us during your Midterm Break~

Our theme for this year's event will be Data Science.

Our Mission

We want to provide a meaningful experience to you through our workshops, mentorship, and resources that your team will need in order to build your project(s).

We also off you the chance to grow your network within the tech industry by connecting with our sponsors and meeting fellow hackers!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an opportunity to learn new concepts about tech and apply them to some cool ideas. These ideas are formulated to create things like websites, apps, or unthinkable solutions to a problem presented by the theme of the hackathon.

Don't worry, you don't have to have experience coding or be an expert with computers to participate, think of it as an opportunity!

Who can register?

Whether you're a high-school student, a university student, or a recent gradate, you can definitely register!

Mentor and judge opportunities are also open for experienced professionals interested in our event! Email us at operations@hackfrostnl.ca

Do I need a team?

Yes, we strongly encourage you to form a team of 2 to 4 people before registering. When you register, you can create a team, join an already existing one, or sign up individually if you feel like flying solo!

What do I need to register?

Just yourself and access to a computer and internet.

Hack Frost NL is FREE for all participants

What if I don't code? Should I still register?

Of course! The event isn't about who the best programmer is, its a learning experience! We will run workshops for beginners throughout the event that will help you learn something valuable!

What about the prizes?

We're currently looking for cool prizes that will offer winners valuable experiences. Stay tuned!

Would I get swag and merchandise?

Yes and yes! We're working hard to bring you cool swag that will get you right in the hacker mood. You'll either be able to pick up your swag or have it delivered to your door.

If you'd like to receive our swag, please fill in your shipping information when you register for the event.
