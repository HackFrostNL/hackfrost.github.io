Welcome to Hack Frost!

Hack Frost NL is a tech hackathon for students in the community organized by students from Memorial University of Newfoundland. We invite students to participate in an exciting weekend to learn new skills and connect with a community of tech enthusiasts. No matter your experience level, this hackathon is for you! Come chill with us during your Midterm Break.

For this year's event, we encourage students to harness Data Science in order to develop a solution addressing one of many real-world challenges posed by COVID-19. More information, including the challenge prompt, will be announced at the beginning of the hackathon.


Our Mission

We want to provide a meaningful experience to you through our workshops, mentorship, and resources that your team will need in order to build your project(s). We also offer you opportunities to grow your network within the tech industry by connecting with our sponsors and meeting fellow hackers!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an opportunity to learn new concepts about tech and apply them to some cool ideas. These ideas are formulated to create things like websites, apps, or unthinkable solutions to a problem presented by the theme of the hackathon.

Don't worry, you don't have to have experience coding or be an expert with computers to participate, think of it as an opportunity!

Who can register?

Whether you're a high school, college, or university student, or even a recent graduate, you can definitely register!

Mentor and judge opportunities are also open for experienced professionals interested in our event! Email us at operations@hackfrostnl.ca

Do I need a team?

Yes. We strongly encourage you to arrange a team of 2 to 4 people before registering. When you register, you can create a team, join an already existing one, or sign up individually.

What do I need to register?

Just yourself and access to a computer and internet.

What if I don't code? Should I still register?

Of course! The event isn't about who the best programmer is, it's a learning experience! We will run workshops for beginners throughout the event that will help you learn something valuable!

What about the prizes?

We're currently looking for cool prizes that will offer winners valuable experiences. Stay tuned!

Would I get swag and merchandise?

Yes and yes! We're working hard to bring you cool swag that will get you right in the hacker mood. Swag will be either pickup or will be delivered right to your door. If you would like to receive swag, please enter your shipping address when signing up.
