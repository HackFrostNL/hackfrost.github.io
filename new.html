Welcome to Hack Frost!

Hack Frost NL is a university-wide hackathon organized by students of Memorial University of Newfoundland. We invite students to participate in an exciting weekend to learning new skills and connect with a community of tech enthusiasts! Whether you're a noob or a seasoned programmer, everyone is welcome. Come chill with us!

Our theme for this year's event will be Data Science. More info coming soon...

Our Mission

We want to provide a meaningful experience to you through our workshops, mentorship, and resources that your team will need in order to build your project(s). We also want our participants to get the chance to grow their network within the tech industry through our sponsors.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an opportunity to learn new concepts about tech and apply them to some cool ideas. These ideas are formulated to create things like websites, apps, or unthinkable solutions to a problem presented by the theme of the hackathon.

Don't worry, you don't have to have experience coding or be an expert with computers to participate, think of it as an opportunity!

Who can register?

Whether you're a high-school student, a university student, or a recent gradate, you can definitely register!

Mentor and judge opportunities are also open for experienced professionals interested in our event!

Do I need a team?

Not at all, but there's nothing better than working in a team, right? We encourage you to form a team of up to 4 people, but if you're confident in your skills or feel that you work better alone, feel free to fly solo!

What do I need to register?

Just yourself and access to a computer and internet.

Hack Frost NL is FREE for all participants

What if I don't code? Should I still register?

Of course! The event isn't about who's the best programmer, its a learning experience! We will run workshops for beginners throughout the event that will help you learn something valuable!

There will also be a dedicated prize category for first-time programmers, provided they present a working prototype!

What about the prizes?

We're currently looking for cool prizes that will offer winners valuable experiences. Stay tuned!

What I get swag and merchandise?

Yes and yes! We're working hard to bring you cool swag that will get you right in the hacker mood. We'll keep you posted!
